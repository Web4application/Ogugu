# Discord Activities

This section lists all available activities you can run in your Discord server.

## Activities
- **Game Activity** – Play mini-games in voice channels.
- **Music Activity** – Listen and share music.
- **Watch Together Activity** – Watch videos together.
- **Study / Focus Activity** – Collaborative focus sessions.
- **Trivia / Quiz Activity** – Compete with friends in quizzes.
- **Drawing / Art Activity** – Create art collaboratively.
- **Voice Chat Games** – Fun icebreaker games in voice.
- **Polls & Votes Activity** – Engage server members with polls.
- **Coding / Dev Activity** – Interactive coding challenges.
- **Custom Bot Games** – Any unique game your bot can handle.
